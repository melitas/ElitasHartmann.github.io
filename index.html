Elitas Hartmann Lab

Single-Cell Microengineering of Stress and Adaptation

We engineer microfluidic and bioelectronic platforms to quantify how single cells respond to stress in disease and infection.

Research Programs

Antibiotic Resistance & Bacterial Persistence

Microfluidic microecologies and single-cell assays to understand bacterial survival and tolerance under antibiotic stress.

Cancer–Immune Microenvironments

Engineered tumor–immune microenvironments to quantify cell–cell communication, migration, and phenotypic heterogeneity.

Stress-Based Biosensors & Diagnostics

Portable and low-cost biosensing platforms for stress-responsive diagnostics.
