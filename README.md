# Elitas Hartmann Lab
### Single-Cell Microengineering of Stress and Adaptation

We engineer microfluidic and bioelectronic platforms to quantify how single cells respond to stress in disease and infection.

---

## Research Programs

**Antibiotic Resistance & Bacterial Persistence**  
Microfluidic microecologies and single-cell assays to quantify tolerance and survival under antibiotic stress.  
[Learn more]({{ "/research/#antibiotic" | relative_url }})

**Cancer–Immune Microenvironments**  
Microengineered platforms to study tumor–immune communication, migration, and phenotypic heterogeneity.  
[Learn more]({{ "/research/#cancer" | relative_url }})

**Stress-Based Biosensors & Diagnostics**  
Portable biosensing and nucleic-acid testing systems for resource-limited and field settings.  
[Learn more]({{ "/research/#biosensors" | relative_url }})
